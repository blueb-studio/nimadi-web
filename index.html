About Nimadi Farms

From Soil
to Your Table.

Nimadi Farms is rooted in the ancient farmlands of Nimad, Madhya Pradesh — a region known for its fertile black soil and centuries-old agricultural wisdom. We grow, process, and deliver food the way it was always meant to be: honest, clean, and full of life.

Every jar of ghee we clarify, every bottle of oil we cold-press, and every batch of atta we stone-grind carries the same promise — nothing added, everything preserved. No shortcuts. No compromises. Just pure nourishment from farm to family.
